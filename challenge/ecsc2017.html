ECSC2017 was taking place from Oct 30th to Nov the 3rd in Málaga, Spain.

The ECSC is a chance for the top cyber security talents in Europe to cooperate, meet and compete against each other with the aim to improve their skills in a high-level environment. The ECSC aims at enhancing cyber security talent across Europe and connecting high potentials with industry leading organizations.

See web site of the European Cyber Security Challenges 2017
