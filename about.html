About Us

The Swiss Cyber Storm is a non profit organization hosting the international Swiss Cyber Storm Conference and running the Swiss Hacking Challenge - the Swiss Qualifying for the European Cyber Security Challenges - with a strong focus of identifying and supporting young cyber talents.
