Calendar 2018

  1. Swiss Hacking Challenge (April 1st 12:00h - July 31th 24:00h 2018)
    Test your skill on the Online Qualification. It's free. The 30 best participants will be invited to the Swiss Final.

  2. Swiss Final (Beginning of September 2018)
    The top 30 cyber talents from the Swiss Online Qualification will be invited to the Swiss Final at the Campus Sursee in Lucerne. You will meet other cyber talents and will gain experience by solving challenges (see Swiss Final 2017).

  3. European Final In London UK (October 14th - October 18th 2018)
    The top 10 participants of the Swiss Finals will have the opportunity to join the Swiss national team, representing Switzerland at the ECSC (European Cyber Security Challenge) in London 2018. What a thrilling outlook!

  4. Swiss Cyber Storm Conference (October 30th 2018)
    The top cyber talents from the Swiss Final will be invited to the annual Swiss Cyber Storm Conference at the Kursaal in Berne. You will have the opportunity to meet security professionals and do some training and education.
