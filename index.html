Switzerland is seeking new cyber security talents. Join the Swiss Hacking Challenge 2018 and qualify for this year’s Swiss Master Final and possibly even for the European Cyber Security Challenge Final in London, UK.

Since 2013, Swiss Cyber Storm is running the official Swiss Hacking Challenge and is hereby looking for the next generation of cyber security attackers and defenders. Our competition programme involves online qualifiers and an in-person semi-final culminating in the annual Swiss Master Finale where our champion of the year is crowned.

Registration JUNIORS

Are you between 14 and 20 years old? Are you interested in cyber security? You do not need to be an “expert”, but you must really enjoy digging into computers and networks. Please sign-up in the JUNIOR category .

Registration SENIORS

Are you between 20 and 25 years old? Are you studying at the ETH, EPFL or other Swiss FH Fachhochschule (ZHAW, BFH, Supsi, HTW, HEIG-VD, HSR, …)? Are you already working in a Swiss company and working as a developer, system- or network engineer? Please sign-up for the SENIOR category.

Swiss Master Final

Swiss Cyber Storm is firstly running the Swiss Master Final in parallel to the annual Swiss Cyber Storm Security Conference. 30 cyber talents from the online phase are invited to fight for the Swiss Master (junior and senior). This is an awesome opportunity for you to get acknowledged by the Swiss industry as the best “hacker” of our country.

European Cyber Security Challenge

Cyber Security Challenge UK is running this year’s European Cyber Security Challenge Final in London UK. Every country must have a team of 10 players with 5 from the JUNIOR and 5 from the SENIOR age category. Switzerland has been battling in this competition since the beginning. This is an extraordinary opportunity for cyber talents to meet and compete against other European players.

About Swiss Cyber Storm

The non-profit organization “Swiss Cyber Storm” is running the annual Swiss Hacking Contest. Players of the competition are not expected to pay any costs for taking part of the Swiss Master Final or European Cyber Security Challenge Final, except for travelling within Switzerland to the trainings and competitions. Swiss Cyber Storm takes care of the costs (flight, hotel and food).
